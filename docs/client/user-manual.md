## Getting Started

### What is the E-Governance Complaint Management System?

The E-Governance Complaint Management System is a digital platform that allows citizens to report municipal issues like water supply problems, road repairs, garbage collection, and other civic concerns. The system helps track these complaints from submission to resolution, ensuring transparency and accountability.

### Who Can Use This System?

- **Citizens**: Registered users who can submit and track complaints
- **Guest Users**: Anyone can submit complaints without registration
- **Ward Officers**: Municipal officers who manage complaints in their areas
- **Maintenance Teams**: Teams responsible for resolving issues
- **Administrators**: System managers who oversee the entire process

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. Visit the website: `https://your-city-complaints.gov.in`
3. The system works on computers, tablets, and smartphones

### System Languages

The system is available in multiple languages:
- English
- Hindi (हिंदी)
- Malayalam (മലയാളം)

To change language, click the language selector in the top-right corner.

## For Citizens

### Creating Your Account

#### Step 1: Registration
1. Click the **"Register"** button on the homepage
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name**: Your complete name as it appears on official documents
   - **Email Address**: A valid email you check regularly
   - **Phone Number**: Your mobile number with country code (+91 for India)
   - **Password**: Choose a strong password with at least 8 characters
   - **Ward**: Select your residential ward from the dropdown
3. Click **"Register"** to create your account
4. Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account

#### Step 2: First Login
1. Click **"Login"** on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be taken to your personal dashboard

### Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:

- **Quick Stats**: Number of your complaints by status
- **Recent Complaints**: Your latest submitted complaints
- **Quick Actions**: Buttons to submit new complaints or view all complaints
- **Notifications**: Important updates about your complaints

### Submitting a Complaint

#### Step 1: Start New Complaint
1. From your dashboard, click **"Submit New Complaint"**
2. Or navigate to **"Complaints"** > **"Create New"** from the menu

#### Step 2: Fill Complaint Details
**Basic Information:**
- **Title**: Write a clear, brief title (e.g., "Water supply disruption in Building A")
- **Description**: Provide detailed information about the issue
- **Complaint Type**: Select from options like:
  - Water Supply
  - Electricity
  - Road Repair
  - Garbage Collection
  - Street Lighting
  - Sewerage
  - Public Health
  - Traffic
  - Other

**Priority Level:**
- **Low**: Minor issues that don't affect daily life significantly
- **Medium**: Issues that cause some inconvenience
- **High**: Issues that seriously affect daily life
- **Critical**: Urgent issues affecting health, safety, or security

**Location Details:**
- **Ward**: Your ward (usually pre-filled from your profile)
- **Area**: Specific area or neighborhood
- **Address**: Complete address where the issue is located
- **Landmark**: Nearby landmarks to help locate the issue (optional)

**Contact Information:**
- **Phone**: Your contact number
- **Email**: Your email address (usually pre-filled)

#### Step 3: Add Supporting Evidence
- Click **"Upload Files"** to add photos, videos, or documents
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF (up to 10MB per file)
- Good photos help resolve issues faster

#### Step 4: Submit Complaint
1. Review all information carefully
2. Click **"Submit Complaint"**
3. You'll receive a confirmation with your complaint ID
4. An email confirmation will be sent to your registered email

### Tracking Your Complaints

#### View All Complaints
1. Go to **"Complaints"** from the main menu
2. You'll see a list of all your complaints with:
   - Complaint title and type
   - Current status
   - Submission date
   - Priority level

#### Filter and Search
- Use the **Status filter** to see complaints by status (Registered, Assigned, In Progress, Resolved, Closed)
- Use the **Type filter** to see specific types of complaints
- Use the **Search box** to find complaints by keywords

#### View Complaint Details
1. Click on any complaint from your list
2. You'll see complete details including:
   - Full description and location
   - Current status and progress
   - Assigned officer/team (if any)
   - Status history timeline
   - Any updates or comments from officials
   - Attached files

### Understanding Complaint Status

- **Registered**: Your complaint has been received and is in the queue
- **Assigned**: A ward officer has been assigned to handle your complaint
- **In Progress**: Work on your complaint has started
- **Resolved**: The issue has been fixed
- **Closed**: The complaint process is complete
- **Reopened**: Complaint was reopened for additional work

### Providing Feedback

Once your complaint is resolved:
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Visit your complaint details page
3. You'll see a **"Provide Feedback"** section
4. Rate the service (1-5 stars)
5. Write comments about the resolution quality
6. Click **"Submit Feedback"**

Your feedback helps improve municipal services.

### Managing Your Profile

#### Update Personal Information
1. Click your name in the top-right corner
2. Select **"Profile"**
3. Click **"Edit Profile"**
4. Update your information
5. Click **"Save Changes"**

#### Change Password
1. Go to **"Profile"** > **"Security"**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password twice
5. Click **"Update Password"**

#### Notification Preferences
1. Go to **"Profile"** > **"Settings"**
2. Choose how you want to receive notifications:
   - Email notifications
   - SMS notifications (if available)
   - Notification frequency

## For Guest Users

### What is Guest Access?

Guest access allows you to submit complaints without creating an account. This is useful for:
- Visitors to the city
- People who don't want to create accounts
- Emergency situations requiring quick reporting

### Submitting a Guest Complaint

#### Step 1: Access Guest Form
1. On the homepage, click **"Submit Complaint as Guest"**
2. Or click **"Guest"** in the top menu

#### Step 2: Fill Personal Information
- **Full Name**: Your complete name
- **Email Address**: Valid email for communication
- **Phone Number**: Contact number with country code

#### Step 3: Fill Complaint Details
Fill the same information as registered users:
- Title and description
- Complaint type and priority
- Location details

#### Step 4: Email Verification
1. Click **"Submit Complaint"**
2. Check your email for a verification code (OTP)
3. Enter the 6-digit code in the system
4. Click **"Verify"**

#### Step 5: Get Tracking Information
After verification:
- You'll receive a unique tracking number (e.g., CSC2024011501234)
- Save this number to track your complaint
- You'll get an email with tracking instructions

### Tracking Guest Complaints

#### Using Tracking Number
1. Visit the **"Track Complaint"** page
2. Enter your tracking number
3. Enter your email address
4. Click **"Track Complaint"**
5. View your complaint status and progress

#### Converting to Registered User
After submitting a guest complaint, you can:
1. Click **"Create Account"** in the confirmation message
2. Your information will be pre-filled
3. Add a password to complete registration
4. Your guest complaint will be linked to your new account

## For Ward Officers

### Ward Officer Responsibilities

Ward Officers are responsible for:
- Managing complaints in their assigned ward
- Assigning complaints to maintenance teams
- Monitoring complaint resolution progress
- Communicating with citizens about complaint status
- Ensuring SLA (Service Level Agreement) compliance

### Ward Officer Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Ward Statistics**: Total complaints, resolved complaints, pending complaints
- **Recent Activity**: Latest complaint updates in your ward
- **SLA Alerts**: Complaints approaching deadlines
- **Team Performance**: Status of maintenance teams

### Managing Complaints

#### Viewing Ward Complaints
1. Go to **"Complaints"** from the main menu
2. Complaints are automatically filtered to show only your ward
3. Use additional filters for status, type, or priority

#### Reviewing New Complaints
1. Click on a complaint with "Registered" status
2. Review all details carefully:
   - Location accuracy
   - Issue description
   - Priority level
   - Supporting evidence
3. Verify the complaint is valid and within your ward

#### Assigning Complaints
1. Open a complaint that needs assignment
2. Click **"Assign Complaint"**
3. Select appropriate maintenance team or individual
4. Add assignment notes if needed
5. Click **"Assign"**
6. The status changes to "Assigned"

#### Updating Complaint Status
1. Open any assigned complaint
2. Click **"Update Status"**
3. Select new status from dropdown
4. Add comments explaining the update
5. If resolving, add resolution details
6. Click **"Update Status"**

#### Communication with Citizens
- Always add meaningful comments when updating status
- Be specific about what actions are being taken
- Provide estimated timelines when possible
- Respond to citizen inquiries promptly

### SLA Management

#### Understanding SLAs
Each complaint type has a Service Level Agreement (SLA):
- **Water Supply**: 48 hours
- **Electricity**: 24 hours
- **Road Repair**: 7 days
- **Garbage Collection**: 24 hours
- **Street Lighting**: 72 hours

#### SLA Monitoring
1. Go to **"Reports"** > **"SLA Dashboard"**
2. View complaints by SLA status:
   - **Green**: Well within SLA
   - **Yellow**: Approaching SLA deadline
   - **Red**: SLA deadline exceeded

#### Escalation Process
For complaints exceeding SLA:
1. Review the complaint urgently
2. Reallocate resources if needed
3. Provide updated timeline to citizen
4. Escalate to administrator if required

### Team Coordination

#### Managing Maintenance Teams
1. Go to **"Teams"** section
2. View team workload and availability
3. Distribute complaints evenly among teams
4. Monitor team performance metrics

#### Communication Tools
- Use internal messaging for team coordination
- Schedule field visits for complex issues
- Share updates with relevant stakeholders

## For Maintenance Teams

### Maintenance Team Responsibilities

Maintenance teams are responsible for:
- Receiving and acknowledging assigned complaints
- Conducting field visits to assess issues
- Performing necessary repairs or maintenance
- Updating complaint status with progress
- Documenting work completion with evidence

### Maintenance Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:
- **Assigned Tasks**: Complaints assigned to you or your team
- **Task Priority**: Urgent tasks requiring immediate attention
- **Today's Schedule**: Planned visits and activities
- **Completion Metrics**: Your performance statistics

### Managing Assigned Tasks

#### Viewing Assigned Complaints
1. Go to **"My Tasks"** from the main menu
2. See all complaints assigned to you
3. Tasks are sorted by priority and SLA deadline

#### Acknowledging Assignment
1. Open a newly assigned complaint
2. Review all details and location
3. Click **"Acknowledge Task"**
4. Add any initial observations
5. Update expected completion time if needed

#### Updating Progress
1. Open an active complaint
2. Click **"Update Progress"**
3. Select appropriate status:
   - **In Progress**: Work has started
   - **Pending Parts**: Waiting for materials/equipment
   - **Resolved**: Work is completed
4. Add detailed comments about work performed
5. Upload photos of completed work (for resolved complaints)

### Field Work Documentation

#### Before Starting Work
1. Take photos of the issue
2. Note any safety concerns
3. Identify required materials/tools
4. Update status to "In Progress"

#### During Work
1. Document any challenges encountered
2. If additional resources are needed, update complaint with requirements
3. Communicate delays promptly

#### After Completion
1. Take photos of completed work
2. Update status to "Resolved"
3. Add completion notes describing work done
4. Include any recommendations for prevention

### Communication

#### With Ward Officers
- Report any issues requiring escalation
- Request additional resources when needed
- Provide regular updates on complex tasks

#### With Citizens (if allowed)
- Be professional and courteous
- Explain work being performed
- Provide realistic timelines

### Mobile App Usage

Many maintenance teams use mobile phones for field work:
1. Access the system through your mobile browser
2. Use camera to upload photos directly
3. Update complaint status from the field
4. Access complaint details offline (cached)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Cannot Log In
**Problem**: "Invalid email or password" error
**Solutions**:
1. Check if Caps Lock is on
2. Verify email spelling
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Contact administrator if account is locked

#### Forgot Password
**Steps**:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click the link and create new password
5. Log in with new password

#### Cannot Submit Complaint
**Possible Issues**:
- Required fields not filled
- File size too large (max 10MB)
- Internet connection problems
- Invalid file format

**Solutions**:
1. Check all required fields are completed
2. Reduce file size or use different format
3. Check internet connection
4. Try again later if server is busy

#### Email Not Received
**Check**:
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Email address spelling
3. Email server delays (wait 10-15 minutes)
4. Contact administrator if persistent

#### Cannot Upload Files
**Solutions**:
1. Check file size (must be under 10MB)
2. Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, PDF)
3. Try uploading one file at a time
4. Clear browser cache and try again

#### Slow Performance
**Solutions**:
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check internet connection speed
4. Try during off-peak hours

### Browser Compatibility

#### Recommended Browsers
- **Chrome** 90 or newer
- **Firefox** 88 or newer
- **Safari** 14 or newer
- **Edge** 90 or newer

#### Browser Settings
1. Enable JavaScript
2. Allow cookies
3. Enable location services (for auto-location)
4. Keep browser updated

### Mobile Issues

#### App Crashes or Freezes
1. Close and reopen browser
2. Restart device
3. Clear browser cache
4. Update browser app

#### Camera Not Working
1. Grant camera permissions
2. Check if camera is being used by another app
3. Restart browser
4. Use file upload instead

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is there a mobile app for this system?**
A: Currently, the system works through web browsers on all devices. A dedicated mobile app may be available in the future.

**Q: How much does it cost to use this system?**
A: The system is completely free for all citizens and users.

**Q: Can I submit complaints in languages other than English?**
A: Yes, the system supports multiple languages including Hindi and Malayalam. You can change the language in the settings.

**Q: How long does it take to resolve a complaint?**
A: Resolution time depends on the type of issue. Each complaint type has a Service Level Agreement (SLA) ranging from 24 hours to 7 days.

### Account and Registration

**Q: Do I need to create an account to submit a complaint?**
A: No, you can submit complaints as a guest user. However, having an account provides better tracking and communication features.

**Q: Can I change my ward after registration?**
A: Yes, contact your ward officer or system administrator to update your ward information.

**Q: What if I forget my login details?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" feature on the login page, or contact the system administrator for help.

### Complaint Submission

**Q: What types of issues can I report?**
A: You can report various municipal issues including water supply, electricity, roads, garbage collection, street lighting, sewerage, public health, and traffic problems.

**Q: Can I submit multiple complaints about the same issue?**
A: Please check if a similar complaint already exists before submitting. Duplicate complaints may be merged.

**Q: What should I do if my complaint is urgent?**
A: Mark your complaint as "Critical" priority and include detailed information about why it's urgent. For emergencies, also contact local emergency services.

**Q: Can I edit my complaint after submission?**
A: You cannot edit complaint details after submission, but you can add comments and additional information through the complaint details page.

### Tracking and Updates

**Q: How will I know when my complaint status changes?**
A: You'll receive email notifications for all status updates. You can also check your dashboard or complaint details page anytime.

**Q: What does each status mean?**
A: 
- **Registered**: Complaint received
- **Assigned**: Officer/team assigned
- **In Progress**: Work started
- **Resolved**: Issue fixed
- **Closed**: Process complete

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?**
A: You can provide feedback rating and comments. If unsatisfied, contact the ward officer or administrator for review.

**Q: Can I cancel my complaint?**
A: Contact your ward officer or administrator to discuss complaint cancellation if the issue has been resolved by other means.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The website is not loading properly**
A: Try clearing your browser cache, updating your browser, or using a different browser. Contact support if the problem persists.

**Q: I can't upload photos**
A: Ensure your photos are under 10MB and in JPG, PNG, or PDF format. Check your internet connection and try again.

**Q: The system logged me out unexpectedly**
A: For security, the system logs you out after periods of inactivity. Simply log in again to continue.

### Privacy and Security

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
A: Yes, the system uses encryption and security measures to protect your data. Personal information is only used for complaint processing and communication.

**Q: Who can see my complaints?**
A: Your complaints are visible to you, relevant ward officers, maintenance teams, and administrators. Personal details are protected and not shared publicly.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Contact the system administrator to request account deletion. Note that complaint history may be retained for municipal records.
